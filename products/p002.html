Style Report

Snug and rustic

This season, rich ochre and terracotta bring to mind the colours of falling leaves as we bid a fond farewell to the light and welcome dark, cosy evenings. Applied to rugs, throws and cushions in timeless designs rooted in Swedish traditions, this time of the year feels anything but dull.

Inspired by homes of the past, updated for the future – patterns rooted in Swedish traditions make a bold comeback. They bring colour and a sprinkling of nostalgia, but with a modern look to cushions, rugs and bedding. Some call it timeless design, but we like to think of it more as patterns and designs with no expiry date.

Simple pieces lay the base of both looks
